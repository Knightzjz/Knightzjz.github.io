I'm excited to share that our paper **IMDL-BenCo** has been accepted as a **Spotlight** at NeurIPS 2024! 🎉

## What is IMDL-BenCo?

IMDL-BenCo is a comprehensive benchmark and codebase for **Image Manipulation Detection & Localization**. Key features:

- **Unified evaluation framework** for multiple manipulation types
- **State-of-the-art methods** comparison
- **Open-source codebase** with 7k+ GitHub stars

## Why This Matters

Detecting manipulated images is increasingly important in the age of generative AI. Our benchmark provides:

1. Standardized evaluation protocols
2. Comprehensive dataset coverage
3. Fair comparison across methods
