周吉喆，四川大学计算机学院副教授， 四川省千人， IEEE TETCI 副主编 J. Imaging 客座主编。 ，主要研究方向为伪造图像检测（Fake Image Detection）及生成与取证统一（Forensic-Embedded Generation）。 我主导开发了 IML-ViT Sparse-ViT IMDL-BenCo ForensicHub 等多个开源项目，更多详情请见 GitHub 与 Google Scholar 主页。

I am an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, and Sichuan Provincial Thousand Talents. I serve as Associate Editor of IEEE TETCI and Guest Editor of J. Imaging. My research focuses on Fake Image Detection and Forensic-Embedded Generation. I have led the development of several open-source projects, including IML-ViT Sparse-ViT IMDL-BenCo ForensicHub. For more details, please visit my GitHub and Google Scholar profiles.
